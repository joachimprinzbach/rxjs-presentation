Why RxJS?

Callbacks

  • Event driven
  • Callback hell
  • Tight coupling between source and listener
  • No synchronization

Promise

  • Event driven
  • No callback hell
  • Limited synchronization (Promise.all())
  • Tight coupling between source and listener

asynchronous data streams

actions on the streams

Observables

  • You get control of the streams
  • Decoupling between event source (observable) and listener (subscriber)
  • Part of ReactiveX
  • Not an ES standard yet
    (there is proposal)
